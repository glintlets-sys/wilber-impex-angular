Who We Are

WILBER is a privately-held company that manufactures Stone care products and was established in the year 2010. The company is certified for Quality Management System and accredited with ISO 9001:2015 credentials.

Even though the commencement of the industry was small, it was driven by persistence and optimism. For almost 10 years now, the company has steadily expanded its business in India. With over 100 various products that we produce, we have divided the process of manufacturing into various steps to minimize costs and maximize efficiency, providing a wide range of product offerings.

We constantly work on developing our niches and innovate methods to differentiate ourselves from the competition. Wilber has a strong customer base with more than 400 satisfied customers. The company has experienced robust growth over the last five years, achieving an annual turnover of 15 Crores.

Expertise

Our foremost focus is Customer Delight & Satisfaction.

To be a credible company by the introduction of innovative products with cutting-edge technology and consistent quality. We believe in product value over product volume.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Wilber dealers and products

You can find the nearest Wilber dealer by using our "Find a Dealer" tool on this page. Simply select your state and city from the dropdown menus or use the search feature. You can also contact our customer support team for assistance.

Wilber dealers offer a comprehensive range of tile fixing and stone care products including tile adhesives, tile joint fillers, stone cleaners, stone polishes, and specialized industrial solutions. The product availability may vary by dealer location.

To become a Wilber dealer, you can fill out the dealer inquiry form on this page or contact our business development team. We'll evaluate your application based on market potential, business experience, and other factors before proceeding with the dealership process.

Many Wilber dealers provide installation services or can recommend professional installers who are experienced with Wilber products. Please check with your local dealer for specific services offered in your area.

Wilber dealers are trained to provide technical guidance on product selection and application. For complex projects, our dealers can also arrange for specialized technical support from Wilber's expert team to ensure the best results for your specific requirements.
